This simple carrot recipe contains four simple ingredients, five simple steps, and takes only a minute to prepare. It makes a great side dish or topping for salad.

Carrots do a body good!

Ingredients

  • One carrot
  • 1 tsp. refined coconut oil
  • 1/2 tsp. white vinegar
  • Iodized salt
  1. Clean and peel the carrot.
  2. Using your carrot peeler, grate the carrot length-wise into long thin strips. Grating the carrot this way helps to maximize the absorption of nutrients within the digestive tract.
  3. Transfer the grated carrot into a dish, warm the coconut oil if needed, add the coconut oil to the carrot, and mix.
  4. Salt very lightly, and mix well.
  5. Add the vinegar to the carrot, and mix again.
